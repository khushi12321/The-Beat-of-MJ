Iconic Dance Moves Guide

The Moonwalk

Step 1: Keep one foot flat on the ground
Step 2: Slide the back foot smoothly backward
Step 3: Lift the front foot and repeat the motion
Tip: Practice on smooth surfaces and keep your body upright

Anti-Gravity Lean

Secret: Special shoes with pegs that hook into the stage
Technique: Lean forward at 45-degree angle
Core: Maintain straight spine and engage core muscles
Note: Requires special equipment for the full effect

The Spin

Step 1: Start with feet together
Step 2: Rise up on toes and spin rapidly
Step 3: Keep arms close to body for balance
Tip: Spot a fixed point to prevent dizziness

The Crotch Grab

Timing: Perfectly synchronized with musical beats
Attitude: Confident and bold expression
Context: Part of his powerful stage presence
Legacy: Became one of his most recognizable gestures
